CountriesPortService

SOAP 1.1

A seguir está um exemplo de solicitação e resposta SOAP 1.1. Os marcadores de posição mostrados precisam ser substituídos por valores reais. Países disponíveis: Spain, Poland e United Kingdom

Solicitação: 

POST /ws HTTP/1.1
Host: host:port
Content-Type: text/xml; charset=utf-8
Content-Length: length

<soapenv:Envelope xmlns:soapenv="http://schemas.xmlsoap.org/soap/envelope/" xmlns:gs="http://example.com/contrato">
  <soapenv:Header/>
  <soapenv:Body>
      <gs:getCountryRequest>
          <gs:name>string</gs:name>
      </gs:getCountryRequest>
  </soapenv:Body>
</soapenv:Envelope>

Resposta:

HTTP/1.1 200 OK
Content-Type: text/xml; charset=utf-8
Content-Length: length

<SOAP-ENV:Envelope xmlns:SOAP-ENV="http://schemas.xmlsoap.org/soap/envelope/">
    <SOAP-ENV:Header/>
    <SOAP-ENV:Body>
        <ns2:getCountryResponse xmlns:ns2="http://example.com/contrato">
            <ns2:country>
                <ns2:name>string</ns2:name>
                <ns2:population>number</ns2:population>
                <ns2:capital>string</ns2:capital>
                <ns2:currency>string</ns2:currency>
            </ns2:country>
        </ns2:getCountryResponse>
    </SOAP-ENV:Body>
</SOAP-ENV:Envelope>
